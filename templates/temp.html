About

Find Out More About Us

Welcome to IT Bank - a trusted financial institution that has been serving our community for over 50 years. Our mission is to provide reliable, high-quality banking services to our customers while maintaining a strong commitment to community involvement and social responsibility.

IT Bank was founded in 1971 with the goal of providing personalized banking services to our local community.

Over the years, we have grown to become a respected financial institution, serving customers across the region.

  • Our Products and Services

    Whether you're looking to save for the future, finance a major purchase, or manage your day-to-day finances, we have the products and services to help you achieve your goals.

  • Our Commitment to Customer Service

    At IT Bank, we believe that providing excellent customer service is the key to our success. Our team of experienced bankers is dedicated to serving our customers with professionalism, courtesy, and respect.

Thank you for considering IT Bank for your banking needs. We look forward to serving you and helping you achieve your financial goals.
